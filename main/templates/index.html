About Agripredict
prediction site

Agriculture is the main occupation of India. Hence it is crucial to figure out the necessities that the agricultural sector needs. In this with the help of available information from the official government websites, different patterns have been identified, and a supervised machine learning model is also suggested. It also aims to offer ways to improve the accuracy of machine learning models. This will help the farmers to identify the factors that would increase the yield of crops(rice) and further estimate the price at which their crops will be sold. Python was used for the analysis of the different Machine Learning Algorithms - Multivariate Regression, Decision Tree, Gaussian Naive Bayes, etc, and it was found that Multivariate Regression gives the best and required result with an R square of 0.97 and 0.98 for yield and price calculation for respectively.).
